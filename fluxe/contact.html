What I'm open to

There's no agenda needed. Whether you have a specific project in mind or you're simply curious — a message is a good place to start.

  • Commissions

    If you have a space — a building, a public square, a festival ground — and want something in it that makes people stop and ask how it's possible.

  • Collaborations

    If your work — in art, architecture, engineering, performance or something else entirely — could use an unexpected structural element, let's explore it.

  • Workshops & education

    If you want to understand tensegrity by actually building it — with a class, a team, or a group of curious people.

  • Just curious

    About the code, the process, the physics, or tensegrity in general. No project required. Every conversation about these structures is a good one.
